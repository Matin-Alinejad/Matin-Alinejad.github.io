About Me

I am a final-year Electrical Engineering student at Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, passionate about research in causal inference, machine learning, and control systems.

My research focuses on causal/counterfactual reasoning in large language models, experiment design for causal discovery, and privacy in statistical models and machine learning.

  • Current position: Undergraduate Student, Research Assistant
