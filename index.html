About Me

I am a final-year Electrical Engineering student at Sharif University of Technology with a deep passion for the intersection of causality, machine learning, and control systems. My primary research interest lies in understanding and enhancing the causal reasoning capabilities of AI systems, particularly Large Language Models.

My work involves developing novel frameworks and benchmarks to test and improve how models understand cause-and-effect relationships. I am driven by a curiosity to bridge the gap between statistical machine learning and true causal understanding, with applications ranging from AI safety to robust decision-making in complex environments.
