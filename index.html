About Me

I am a final-year Electrical Engineering student at Sharif University of Technology, passionate about the intersection of causality, machine learning, and control systems. My academic journey is driven by a deep interest in understanding and enhancing the reasoning capabilities of artificial intelligence systems.

My research focuses on bridging the gap between statistical machine learning and true causal understanding, with particular emphasis on developing frameworks that enable AI systems to reason about cause-and-effect relationships effectively.
